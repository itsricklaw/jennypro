Downloading and Installing Jenny MOD

To integrate Jenny MOD into your Minecraft gameplay, please follow our simple installation guide:

  1. Download the MOD: Click on the download link to get the Java file (.jar).

  4. Create Custom Profile: Open CurseForge, create a custom profile for Jenny MOD.
  5. Launch Minecraft: Start Minecraft through CurseForge and enjoy!
